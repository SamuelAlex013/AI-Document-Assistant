# Sample HR Policy Document - Employee Leave

## Leave Policy Overview

### Annual Leave
- All full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of annual leave per year
- Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance
- Submit leave requests through the HR portal or email hr@company.com
- Maximum of 5 consecutive days can be carried over to the next year

### Sick Leave
- Employees receive 10 days of sick leave per year
- No advance notice required for sick leave
- Medical certificate required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days
- Unused sick leave does not carry over

### Personal Leave
- 3 days of personal leave available per year
- Can be used for personal emergencies or family matters
- Requires manager approval
- Must be requested with as much notice as possible

### Maternity/Paternity Leave
- 12 weeks paid maternity leave
- 4 weeks paid paternity leave
- Additional unpaid leave available upon request
- Must notify HR at least 4 weeks before expected leave date

## How to Apply for Leave

1. Log into the HR portal at hr.company.com
2. Navigate to "Leave Requests"
3. Select the type of leave
4. Choose your dates
5. Add a brief description
6. Submit for manager approval
7. You will receive email confirmation once approved

For urgent requests, contact your manager directly and follow up with formal submission.
